About me

I graduated from Fudan University with a Ph.D. in Economics in June, 2021 and started my academic career in East China Normal University in July, 2021. My main research interests are financial markets and their interactions with macro-economic polices. I am fascinated by the features of the Over-the-Counter (OTC) markets and currently devoted most of my time to OTC market research.

Research

My research interests include:

  • Financial Markets

  • Market Microstructure

  • Money Market and Monetary Polices
